Overview

Shotcut is a free and open-source cross-platform video editing application, developed on the MLT Multimedia Framework. It supports video, audio, and image formats via FFmpeg, and uses a timeline for non-linear video editing of multiple tracks that may be composed of various file formats.

System Requirements

  • GENERAL
#Minimum
1Operating system: 32- or 64-bit Windows 7 - 10, Apple macOS 10.10 - 10.15, or 64-bit Linux with at least glibc 2.19
2CPU: x86-64 Intel or AMD; at least one 2 GHz core for SD, 2 cores for HD, and 4 cores for 4K
3GPU: OpenGL 2.0 that works correctly and is compatible. On Windows, you can also use a card with good, compatible DirectX 9 or 11 drivers
4RAM: At least 4 GB for SD, 8 GB for HD, and 16 GB for 4K
5Network: Shotcut does NOT require access to the network to activate, check a subscription, or send usage analytics. However, some links in the Help menu do link out to Shotcut’s web site.
